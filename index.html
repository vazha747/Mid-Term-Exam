Georgia isn't only known for its beauty

Georgian cuisine has been dubbed as the most underrated regional cuisine by Mashable.com. The website penned a piece about the six "delightful regional cuisines that don't get enough love” and Georgian was ranked number one - higher than Belgian, Sephardic, Tex-Mex, Indian-Chinese, and Native American.
